Privacy Police for Password Protector App

About Password Protector :

Password Protector helps you to store all Your password in one secured place on your Smartphone in an Encrypted Format. You have to create a Master Password that will be used for Encrypting all your passwords.( Note if you forgot your master password even you won't be able to Recover any of your Passwords). Even if your Smartphone gets Lost, nobody could steal your Password from the app unless they have the Master Password.


Personal Info :

Password Protector App Does not collect any of Users Personal Information.


Password Storage :

All Passwords of User are only Stored on Users Device(Mobile or Tablet) in an Encrypted Format.


Security :

All Passwords of User are Encrypted using an Strong Cryptographic Encryption Mechanism, we also recoomend User to use strong Master password for enhanced Security.
